Welcome to BVS ( BHARAT VIDYA SAMACHAR )

A learning by doing platform, which provide full-time access for students, teachers, schools, colleges, universtities to compete, interact and lot more with other teachers, schools, colleges, universtities within and with other states too

BVS, promise you the best education support and make each and every student the best, and make them stand as an example to other's.

We provide online rankings to each and every school based on their performance in BVS tests, and we also help parents to track the performance of the school in which their child is studying, or can track any other school's performance on which they are interested in, through BVS platform.

** BVS, plays role of parents for each and every student in tracking and monitoring the capablity of student and assigning tasks.

"Let BVS change the education system, by ranking schools such that each and every schools automatically improve to prove themselves which enfields the feel of competing, innovative thinking, smart logic developments in each and every student for their respective schools."

What we do?

  • Ranking each and every school based on student's performance in BVS tests and taking up tasks of early education monitoring each student in each and every school.

  • Daily Monitoring of Student's Capability and continous qualitative materiality sessions for teachers and assignment allocations for students.

  • We provide a unique and reliable platform where parents, education ministers, or any individual can track the performance of any school in any location.

  • Affiliated Degrees for Older adults who want to continue their education, with a single click sitting at home. Monitoring uneducated adults to get access to basic essential education materials with multiple assigned BVS volunteers.

Why choose us?

  • Because, BVS is a unique platform where students interact, compete, debate and improve their skills with qualitative practices through BVS.
  • We are first platform born, till date who tracks the real time performance of schools and provide access to parents to keep track the performance of the schools before hand.
  • Unique & Innovative techniques to improve education system using technology, which is very useful for students.
  • Provide Real time support from higher officials, government officials, regarding any issues for students within short period of time.
  • Providing Access to older adults to continue their education sitting in home from affiliated universities & monitoring the performances and quick guidance support.

Services that BVS offer

  • We provide acces to real-time generated data, of various schools, colleges, & universities, to various individuals with nomical charge, such that it is very useful and helpful for governments to improve their state in educational sector& prove themselves in the real world and gain best ranking country wide
  • We also provide detailed explanation of data generated, stats & performance of students year wise to schools, with nominal price such that school can have knowledge, about failure of students and can improve their performance continuously.
  • We provide affiliated courses with low and minium fee, making it access anywhere, & anytime.
  • Automatic, Real-time Data Transfer for our monthly, quarterly, yearly subscription customers, such that the data can be used for comparative analytics in future progress predictions innovatively.
